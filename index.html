Hi 👋

I design systems
for complex work.

My work sits across HCI, regulatory UX, procedural cognition, and broader organizational systems. I study how documents, workflows, interfaces, and support structures shape navigation, reasoning, interpretation, and decision-making in high-stakes environments. My current focus is regulatory UX — how procedural environments shape judgment, and how support systems can make that work more navigable.

current build

Support systems for reasoning in procedural work

I explore how metadata layers, workflow visibility, and reasoning scaffolds can make complex work more navigable without replacing human judgment.

bridge

From HCI and UX to broader systems questions

My work begins with usability and interaction, but extends toward larger questions about accountability, infrastructure, closure, and decision processes.

domain

Regulated documentation as a user environment

SOPs, investigations, CAPA records, and FDA-facing documents are not only compliance artifacts. They are environments people inhabit while trying to find, interpret, justify, and act.
